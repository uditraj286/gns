About Our School

Gyan Niketan School, founded in 1986, is a renowned co-educational senior secondary school in Patna, Bihar. Our institution is built on the vision of providing holistic education that nurtures young minds to become responsible and successful global citizens.

Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (C.B.S.E.), Delhi, we offer a comprehensive curriculum from pre-primary to the senior secondary level. Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure provide an ideal environment for learning and growth.
